Tattoo Aftercare

Important Care Instructions

Proper aftercare is essential for your tattoo to heal beautifully. Follow these guidelines carefully.

Do's

  • Keep the tattoo clean and dry
  • Apply recommended ointment 2-3 times daily
  • Wash gently with mild, fragrance-free soap
  • Keep covered for first 2-3 hours
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing
  • Stay hydrated and eat well
  • Protect from sun exposure

Don'ts

  • Don't pick or scratch the tattoo
  • Don't soak in water (pools, baths)
  • Don't expose to direct sunlight
  • Don't apply alcohol or hydrogen peroxide
  • Don't wear tight clothing over tattoo
  • Don't apply excessive ointment
  • Don't touch with dirty hands

Healing Timeline

Days 1-3

Redness, slight swelling, oozing is normal

Days 4-7

Scabbing begins, keep moisturized

Weeks 2-3

Peeling and flaking occurs

Weeks 4-6

Healing complete, color may appear dull

Recommended Products

Aquaphor, A&D Ointment, or fragrance-free lotion after initial healing period.

Warning Signs

Contact us immediately if you experience excessive redness, pus, fever, or severe pain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it hurt?

Pain levels vary depending on placement and individual tolerance. Most clients describe it as manageable discomfort rather than severe pain. We'll work with you to ensure you're as comfortable as possible.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Pricing varies based on size, complexity, and placement. Custom tattoos start at $150/hour. We provide estimates during your free consultation. Some pieces are priced at a flat rate.

Can I bring my own design?

Absolutely! We welcome your design ideas and reference images. Our artists can work with your design or create a custom interpretation that's perfect for your skin.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area. Avoid tight clothing that might rub against the tattoo during and after the session.

What are the age requirements?

You must be 18 years or older with valid ID. We do not tattoo minors, even with parental consent, as per state regulations.

What's your touch-up policy?

We offer free touch-ups within 3 months of your original tattoo. After that period, touch-ups are charged at a reduced hourly rate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments. A deposit is required to secure your appointment.

Can I get tattooed if I'm pregnant?

For safety reasons, we do not tattoo pregnant or nursing individuals. We'll be happy to schedule your appointment after this period.
